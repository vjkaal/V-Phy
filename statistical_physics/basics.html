Basics of Statistical Physics

Statistical Physics: It is the branch of physics, which uses probability and statitics, and particularly mathematical tools to deal with large values and approximations, in solving physical problems.
Statistical Probability: It is defined as th ratio of no of cases in which the event occurs to the total no of trials carried out.
Priory Probability: It is the ratio of no of cases favourable to the cases to the no of equally likely cases.
